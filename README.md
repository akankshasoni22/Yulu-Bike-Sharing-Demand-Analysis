# Yulu Bike Sharing Demand Analysis

## Project Overview

Yulu is India's leading micro-mobility service provider, offering shared electric bicycles for short-distance commuting. This case study analyzes bike rental demand patterns and identifies the key factors influencing customer usage behavior.

The objective is to help Yulu understand demand fluctuations and provide actionable recommendations to improve fleet utilization, customer satisfaction, and business performance.

---

## Business Problem

Yulu has experienced fluctuations in bike rental demand and wants to understand:

- What factors influence bike rental demand?
- Does weather impact bike usage?
- Does seasonality affect customer behavior?
- Is there a significant difference between weekday and weekend rentals?
- How can Yulu optimize operations based on demand patterns?

## Key Findings

### Weather Impact
Weather significantly affects bike rental demand.

Demand trend:

Clear Weather > Cloudy Weather > Light Rain/Snow

### Seasonal Impact
Bike rentals vary significantly across seasons.

Demand trend:

Fall > Summer > Spring > Winter

### Weekday vs Weekend Analysis
No statistically significant difference was found between weekday and weekend rental demand.

### Weather and Season Relationship
Weather conditions and seasons are statistically associated and jointly influence customer demand.

## Business Recommendations

### Demand Forecasting
- Use weather forecasts to predict bike demand.
- Implement dynamic fleet allocation strategies.

### Seasonal Planning
- Increase bike availability during Fall and Summer.
- Run promotional campaigns during Winter and Spring.

### Customer Engagement
- Launch targeted offers during low-demand periods.
- Improve user retention through loyalty programs.

### Operational Efficiency
- Optimize bike distribution across locations.
- Schedule maintenance during low-demand seasons.
